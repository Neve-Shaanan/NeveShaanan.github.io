URBAN PHOTO STORY by Katerina Elior

More than 70 percent of the population of the neigborhood in last 30 years was made up of refugees and work migrants

Chinese workers came to build a Light train — the biggest public transport project in central Israel. The construction started in 2015 and will go on for decades.
So newcomers already made their Chinatown with shops and restaurants on one of main streets of Neve Sha'anan

Cheap and available chemical drugs are a huge problem of the neighborhood. People from all over the city come here because it's easy to get any drugs, and people often use them out in the street — it's so common, no one will even ask.
